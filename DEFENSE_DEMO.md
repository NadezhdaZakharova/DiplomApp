# Defense Demo Scenario

## Demo goal
Show that physical activity drives game progression in an offline-first mobile app.

## 5-minute script
1. Launch app and open `Today` screen.
2. Show baseline metrics: steps, goal, level, XP, weekly challenge.
3. Press `+1000` several times (or walk with sensor enabled) to simulate activity.
4. Show updates on:
   - progress bar to daily goal,
   - XP and level progress,
   - weekly challenge.
5. Open `Story` screen and show unlocked chapter transition.
6. Open `Rewards` screen and show newly unlocked achievement.
7. Open `Stats` and show recent-day trend data.

## Evaluation metrics for report
- D1/D7 retention in pilot users.
- Mean daily steps before/after gamification.
- Share of users who unlock chapter 1 and chapter 3.

## Fallback plan
- If sensor permissions are unavailable in demo environment, use step simulation buttons.
